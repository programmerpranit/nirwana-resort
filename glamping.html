About


Nirwana Stays offer great views of the rugged Sahyadris. The campsite is landscaped in steps, to ensure everyone gets views of the lake.


Your tent is large one with a high ceiling. You will appreciate the space, the elevations, and the layout. Modern design offers a glamping experience with cozy BOUTIQUE INTERIOR with comfort and all the features you'd expect. Inside the tent, you'll have a bed, blankets, pillows, and a solar light.






Highlights

Camp in a comfortable tent with a double bed.

Swim in the lake or dabble your feet in the cool water all day.
